## Getting Started: Sign In / Log In

Before accessing the full features of HobbyistDecals, including custom designs and orders, users are required to sign in.

### Steps to Sign In:

1.  **Locate the Login Button**: Click on the **"Login"** button found in the top navigation bar.
2.  **Choose a Sign-In Method**:
    - **Social Login**: Use **Apple**, **Facebook**, or **Google** for quick access.
    - **Email Login**: Enter your **Email address** and click "Continue".
3.  **No Account?**: If you are new, there is an option to **Sign up** at the bottom of the modal.

## Decal Shop Guide

Explore our extensive catalog at `https://hobbyistdecals.in/decal-shop`.

### Browsing & Navigation

- **Main Sidebar**: Use the "Shop by Category" filter to jump to specific scale types (Aircraft, Armor, Cars, Sci-Fi).
- **Search Bar**: Located in the header.
- **Filter by Rating**: Click buttons to show products by user rating:
  - _All Ratings_
  - _4+ Stars & Up_
  - _3+ Stars & Up_ (and so on)
- **Sorting**: Use the dropdown to organize results by:
  - _Default Sorting_
  - _Sort By Category_
  - _Sort By Top Picks_
  - _Sort By New Arrivals_

### Product Interaction Guide

Click on any product card to view its full details.

#### 1. Configuring Your Decal

Most decals are "Made to Order" based on your specs:

- **Scale**: Choose from standard scales (1/72, 1/48, 1/32, etc.).
  - _Note_: Price updates dynamically based on the scale chosen.
- **Media Type**: Select your preferred material (see "About Our Media" section).
- **Variation**: Some kits offer variant markings (e.g., "D-Day Stripes" vs "Standard").

#### 2. Reviewing Specs

- **Sheet Size**: Check the physical dimensions to ensure it fits your model box/storage.
- **Instructions**: Read any special application notes listed in the description.

#### 3. Quantity Programs

- **Bulk Discounts**: Automatic discounts apply for ordering 5+ sheets of the same design.
- **Stock Status**: "In Stock" items ship in 24h. "Backorder" items take 3-5 days to print.

## Browse by Categories

## Shopping Cart Features

Once you've selected your items, you can manage your order on the **Shopping Cart** page.

### Adding to Cart

1.  Select your **Scale**, **Media**, and **Quantity**.
2.  Click **Add to Cart** or **Buy Now**.

### Managing Your Cart

On the Cart page (`https://hobbyistdecals.in/cart`), you can:

- **Review Items**: Check details like Scale, Material, and Variation.
- **Adjust Quantity**: Use **+** / **-** buttons to update amounts.
- **Remove Items**: Click the trash icon to delete an item.

### Coupon Codes

Have a promo code?

1.  Enter it in the **Coupon Code** field on the right sidebar.
2.  The discount (e.g., `TEST123`) will apply immediately.

### Shipping Calculator

Estimate shipping before checkout:

1.  Enter your **Pincode**.
2.  Click **Calculate**.
3.  Select a shipping partner (e.g., **India Post**, **Amazon Prepaid**, **Xpressbees**) based on price and delivery time.

### Order Summary

Review the cost breakdown:

- **Subtotal**: Item cost.
- **Discounts**: Quantity or Coupon savings.
- **Shipping**: Selected courier fee.
- **Total**: Final payable amount.

Click **Continue to Checkout** when ready.

## Uploading Custom Designs

For custom print requests:

1. Navigate to the **Custom Decal Design** or **Decal Print** page.
2. Fill out the request form with your details.
3. **Upload File**: Click the upload area to select your design file (PDF, AI, PNG, or high-res JPG).
   - _Note_: Ensure your file is at least 300 DPI for best quality.
4. Provide specific instructions in the comments box (e.g., "Print in 1/24 scale").
5. Submit the form. Our team will review it and send you a quote.

## Checkout & Payment

Complete your order by following these steps on the details page.

### 1. Billing Details

Fill in your personal information:

- **Names**: First and Last Name.
- **Contact**: Email and Phone Number.
- **Address**: Street Address, City, State, Postal Code, and Country.

### 2. Shipping Address

- **Same as Billing**: Check the box if your shipping address matches your billing address.
- **Different Address**: Uncheck and enter the new delivery details.

### 3. Loyalty Points

Earn rewards with every purchase!

- **Status Box**: A blue info box displays your current points balance.
- **Redemption**: You need a minimum of **200 points** to start redeeming them for discounts.

### 4. Payment Methods

Select your preferred secure payment option:

- **Payglocal**: Secure payment gateway for credit/debit cards and net banking.
- **PayPal**: Pay using your PayPal account balance or linked cards.

> **Note**: All payments are SSL encrypted for your security.

### 5. Final Review

Check the **Order Summary** on the right before paying:

- Verify product quantities and discounts.
- Ensure the correct shipping method is selected.
- Confirm the **Total** amount.

### 6. Place Order

Click the orange button: **"Complete Order - Pay $X.XX"** to finalize your purchase.

## Tracking Orders

After your order is dispatched:

1. Log in to your account.
2. Go to **Your Orders**.
3. Find your recent order and click **View Details**.
4. You will see the **Tracking Number** and a link to the courier's tracking page.

## About Our Media

We use premium materials to ensure professional results for scale modelers.

### Waterslide Decal Paper

- **Thickness**: **18 Microns** (Ultra-thin).
- **Finish**: Paint-like integration.
- **Use Case**: Complex curves and high-detail models.

### Glossy White Vinyl

- **Weight**: **80gsm**.
- **Features**: Durable, vibrant color reproduction.
- **Use Case**: Projects requiring bold, opaque backgrounds.

### Clear Vinyl

- **Weight**: **80gsm**.
- **Features**: Sleek, transparent finish.
- **Use Case**: Applications where the underlying surface needs to show through.
